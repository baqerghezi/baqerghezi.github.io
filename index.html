About Me

Welcome to my corner of the web! It's great to meet you. My name is Mohammad Al Baqer you can call me Mohammad or Baqer.

I'm currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Electro-Optics right here at the University of Dayton. My world revolves around optical physics, signal processing, and digging into data analysis – basically, I love figuring out how light works and what we can do with it! I enjoy the whole research process, from developing algorithms (mostly using Python and MATLAB) to sharing what I find through publications and conference talks.

Right now, I'm excited about finding a challenging role in research and development where I can jump in and contribute to the cool new things happening in optics and photonics.
